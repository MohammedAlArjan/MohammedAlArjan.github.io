About Me

I'm a penetration tester based in Saudi Arabia with a BSc in Computer Science from Imam Abdulrahman bin Faisal University. I completed the full HTB Academy CPTS path — all 16 modules — covering every phase of a real pentest: recon, web exploitation, post-exploitation, Active Directory attacks, pivoting, and professional reporting.

I actively work through HTB boxes on CPTS-aligned tracks, which means practicing chaining attacks the way they happen in real engagements — not just running individual tools.

My development background (C#, ASP.NET, PHP, Laravel, React) gives me an attacker's edge on web assessments. I understand how applications are built, so I know exactly where they break.

I take structured notes in Obsidian for every module and box, and I write findings in a professional pentest report format — because you can fail CPTS even if you hack everything but write a bad report.
